    One Idiot Fish: This is an endless runner I developed using the Unity engine. In this game one are, as the title states, One Idiot Fish, attempting to escape every predator/obstacle that comes your way. The goal of the game is to see how far you can get before you eventually get eaten.The inspiration of the game came from the hours upon hours of ocean documentaries I had been watching at the time.
